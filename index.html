About Bivy Tech

Bivy Tech is a bold, innovation-driven African tech company committed to developing talent, building solutions, and gamifying learning for deeper impact. Through our three core divisions — Tech, Bootcamp, and Game — we are building a pipeline of confident, capable, and competitive digital professionals ready to shape the future of technology across Africa and beyond.

Bivy Tech exists to unlock Africa's digital potential by creating platforms, programs, and products that inspire, educate, and empower the next generation of tech leaders.

Our Mission

To empower Africa's digital future by training talent, building solutions, and creating engaging platforms that drive innovation, growth, and real-world impact.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most impactful tech-driven company, inspiring talent, shaping industries, and redefining how tech is learned, lived, and led.

Our Three Core Divisions

Each division serves a unique purpose in our mission to transform Africa's digital landscape.

Tech Division

To design and deliver innovative digital products and solutions that solve real-world problems and improve lives through technology, creating opportunities for growth and development.

Bootcamp Division

To train aspiring tech professionals through hands-on, mentor-led learning in six key tracks: Frontend Development, Backend Development, Product Management, Product Design, Data Analysis, and DevOps.

Game Division

To make tech learning more engaging and inclusive through gamified content, competitions, and community challenges that attract and retain young African talent.
